How I feel about designing

September 27, 2025 by Emeika Osbourne

As someone who was always fascinated with the desgin of websites, whether for fun or for making the website more accessible to its users. I believe every desgin choice serves a purpose. There is a website I used to always go on called RandomWebsites. You would basically just hit a red button on the site, and that button would randomly take you a different website. You'd look at some of them and wonder "Why did they even make this website"

Then you start to realize, there are many times when you truly love doing something, you just do it without having no other reason but because it's fun for you, or relaxing. Some of the sites were silly and some were actually old sites that seemed no longer in use. As silly as it sounds, I would love to make a website one day so that it could end up on that site too.
